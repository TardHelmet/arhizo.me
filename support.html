Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an Account?

To create an Account, download the Smashing Friends Application and click on the "Sign Up" button. You will be asked to provide Your email address and create a password. Follow the instructions to complete Your registration.

How can I reset my password?

If You have forgotten Your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter Your registered email address, and We will send You instructions on how to reset Your password.

Is my Personal Data secure?

We take the security of Your Personal Data seriously. We implement commercially acceptable means to protect Your Personal Data, though no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. For more information, please refer to Our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my Account?

To delete Your Account, go to Your Account settings and select the "Delete Account" option. Please note that certain information may be retained as required by law. For more information about data retention, please refer to Our Privacy Policy.

How can I report inappropriate content or behavior?

If You encounter inappropriate content or behavior while using Our Service, please use the "Report" button within the Application or contact Our support team directly using the form above. We take all reports seriously and will investigate each case promptly.
